WELCOME TO THE COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT PRACTITIONER VERIFICATION DATABASE

Important notices:


1. The purpose of the verification process is solely for the development of a voters roll, which will be used for the establishment
    of a professional board for Community Development Practitioners.
2. The verification system will not process practitioner information, if any outstanding mandatory fields are detected.
3. All practitioners will be issued with a unique reference number (via SMS and e-mail) on submission of the verification form.

All practitioners are requested to complete the following mandatory fields:


1. Personal information
2. Contact information
3. Demographic information
4. Academic information
5. Employment information

Please take note of the following:


1. The submission of Supporting Documents is optional, but Community Development Practitioners who are able to immediately
    submit Supporting Documents are invited to do so. Upload of Supporting Documents should not at any point in
    time delay submission of mandatory information.
2. Provision was made for Community Development Practitioners to submit and upload Supporting Documents at any given time
    after the submission of the listed mandatory information.
